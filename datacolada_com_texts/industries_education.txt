Education providers of all sizes are facing new challenges
Whether you’re a large university, a small RTO or something in between –you are facing more challenges than ever before. Competition is growing, retention is challenging, and student expectations are higher than ever. Students expect you to provide seamless, personalised experiences on whichever channel they choose to engage.
Salesforce Education Cloud offers a unified solution
The Salesforce Education Cloud allows you to build a fully Connected Campus. With the purpose-built Education Data Architecture (EDA), Salesforce is driving transformation in the education sector.
DataColada helps you delight students across the full student life cycle
DataColada helps Australian education providers supercharge their Salesforce investment to build stronger, more personal relationships with students. Whether you need help with recruitment to enrolment, course progression, extra-curricular engagement or alumni relations, we provide the expertise and guidance to help you achieve success with Salesforce.
