Nonprofit competition is growing
The not-for-profit sector in Australia is growing faster than ever before. It is great to see so much participation but how does your stay competitive? Smart nonprofits are using DataColada and Salesforce stand out from the crowd.
An experienced and dedicated nonprofit team
At DataColada, we have an internal practice dedicated to the not-for-profit sector with years of experience building solutions on the Salesforce Nonprofit Success Pack (NPSP). In fact, over 70% of our customers are nonprofit organisations.
Creating value for our nonprofit customers
We understand your challenges and know that you are operating within tight budgets. Our team of experienced nonprofit specialists will help you use the power of Salesforce to meet your mission. We want to be the partner that helps you better serve your constituents and increase your impact.
